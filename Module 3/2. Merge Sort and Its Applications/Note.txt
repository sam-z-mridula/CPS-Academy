/----------------- Divide & Conquer ---------------/
Steps:
    1) Divide the problem into smaller ranges (recursively).
    2) Solve the smaller problems.
    3) Merge the smaller solutions to get the large solution.

⭐ We can merge 2 sorted arrays in O(n) operations.
⭐ Time Complexity of Merge Sort = N*log(N).


🚩Inversion:
In an array, 
    1) i < j        --> i = left, j = right
    2) a[i] > a[j]  --> descendingly 

⭐ Sub-sequence V/S Sub-array
--> Sub-array = Strictly contiguous sub-segment of the array.
--> Sub-sequence = Not necessarily contiguous.

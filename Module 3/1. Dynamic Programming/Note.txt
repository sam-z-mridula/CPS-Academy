***Dynamic Programming***
1. Array to store preCalculated answer.
2. Use preCalculated answer.

*** Theoretical Characteristics of DP ***
1. Dependency: 
    f(n) = f(n-1) + f(n-2)
    Here, f(n) is dependent on f(n-1) and f(n-2).

2. Dynamic Programming has 2 styles:
    1. Recursive
    2. Iterative

        Recursive                    |          Iterative
    1. Uses Recursion.               |       1. Uses for loop.
    2. Top to Bottom Approach.       |       2. Bottom to Top Approach.
       --> বড় থেকে ছোট                         --> ছোট থেকে বড়
    3. Easier.                       |       3. Harder.
    4. Slower. (waiting)             |       4. Faster (No Wating)
                    5. Both works in the same Time Complexity.

*** Basic DP তে সাধারণত যা আসে ***

2. count (১টা কাজ কতভাবে করা যায়)
    --> f(n) = number of ways to reach 'n'

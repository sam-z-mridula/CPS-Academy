**Some built-in Functions:**
1) isalpha (a-z, A-Z), isupper, islower,
2) toupper, tolower 
3) isdigit
4) isspace (newline (\n), space both)

5) strcpy(destination, source) --> Syntax (to copy the 'source' into the 'destination')
6) strcat(destination, source) --> (to concatenate(add) the 'source' at the end of the 'destination' into the 'destination')
7) strcmp(string1, string2)
    --> -1 = string1 ছোট
    --> 1 = string1 বড় 
    --> 0 = সমান (২টা exactly same)
